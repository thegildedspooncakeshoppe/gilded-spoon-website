A Map for Your Senses

This is not a textbook, and there are no grades. Think of this journal as a map. Start where you are, and let your senses guide the way.

Volume I

The Sensory Engine

The Permission Slip. Before you design, you must arrive. This section explores the "Why" behind the cake and gives you the freedom to be messy. It teaches you the language of your own senses before you ever pick up a pencil.

Volume II

The 5-Layer Framework

The Blueprint. Don't just bake; design. This is your guide for turning abstract feelings into tangible elements. You'll learn to translate emotion into Color, Soak, Texture, Placement, and Expression.

Volume III

The Sensory Rolodex

The Toolbelt. Located in the center with a distinct gold border, this is your command center. It holds your Flavor Codex, Color Palettes, and the Emergency Kit for when you have zero spoons left.

Meet Dimple

Dimple Patel McKinney is the Founder of The Gilded Spoon Cake Shoppe & Atelier, a former Nurse, and a neurodivergent creative.

After years of treating baking as chemistry, she discovered it was actually "emotional architecture." Now, she invites you to stop performing and start feeling, using cake as a language for emotional regulation.
