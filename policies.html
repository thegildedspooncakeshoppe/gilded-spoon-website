Store Policies & Fulfillment

How we deliver the tools for your sensory journey.

National Shipping (Physical Goods)

Our non-edible physical artifacts—including My Edible Autobiography Guided Journal and our Sanctuary Box Kits—are available for shipping across the United States.

  • ✔ Carriers: We utilize USPS and UPS to ensure your items arrive safely.
  • ✔ Processing Time: Orders are typically prepared and shipped within 3-5 business days.
  • ✔ Tracking: You will receive a tracking number via email as soon as your package begins its journey.

Digital Downloads

Products marked as "Digital Download" (such as The Architecture of Cake PDF) are delivered instantly via email upon purchase. Please ensure you enter the correct email address at checkout. If you do not receive your file within 15 minutes, please check your spam folder or contact us.

Local Events & Edible Components

Cottage Food Disclosure

ALL EDIBLE PRODUCTS ARE PRODUCED IN A PRIVATE RESIDENCE THAT IS NOT SUBJECT TO GOVERNMENTAL LICENSING OR INSPECTION.

Keep food refrigerated or frozen until prepared for consumption.

Due to Texas Cottage Food Law, any order containing edible/perishable elements (such as party packages) must be personally delivered or picked up. We cannot ship food items via third-party carriers. Local services are available within a 50-mile radius of McKinney, TX.

Returns & Refunds

Digital Products & Consultations

Because of the immediate nature of digital downloads and the reserved time of 1:1 consultations, all sales on digital PDFs, digital templates, and Somatic Audits are final and non-refundable.

Physical Journals & Non-Perishables

Unused and unopened journals or box kits may be returned within 14 days of receipt. Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer. Please contact us to initiate a return.
