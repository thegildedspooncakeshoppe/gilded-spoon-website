Shipping & Fulfillment

How we deliver the tools for your sensory journey.

National Shipping

Our non-edible artifacts—including My Edible Autobiography Guided Journal and our Sanctuary Bundle Kits (non-perishable components)—are available for shipping across the United States.

  • 📦 Carriers: We utilize USPS and UPS to ensure your items arrive safely.
  • 📦 Processing Time: Orders are typically prepared and shipped within 3-5 business days.
  • 📦 Tracking: You will receive a tracking number via email as soon as your package begins its journey.

Local Delivery (Edible Compositions)

Cottage Food Disclosure

ALL EDIBLE PRODUCTS ARE PRODUCED IN A PRIVATE RESIDENCE THAT IS NOT SUBJECT TO GOVERNMENTAL LICENSING OR INSPECTION.

SAFE HANDLING:

Keep food refrigerated or frozen until prepared for consumption.

Due to Texas Cottage Food Law, any order containing edible/perishable elements must be personally delivered or picked up. We cannot ship food items via third-party carriers.

  • 🥄 Service Area: Local delivery is available within a 50-mile radius of McKinney, TX.
  • 🥄 Delivery Schedule: Typically Thursdays between 1 PM and 5 PM.

Atelier Pickup

For our local guests, we offer a dedicated pickup option. We will inform you of the pickup location address in McKinney and coordinate a specific pickup time once your order is confirmed.

Returns & Refunds

Edible Artifacts

All sales are final for edible compositions and perishable items. We cannot accept returns on food products.

Journals & Non-Perishables

Unused and unopened journals or tools may be returned within 14 days of receipt. Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer.
