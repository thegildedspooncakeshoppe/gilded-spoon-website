Our Signature Flavors

Available year-round, these are our timeless classics and most-loved creations.

Gilded Vanilla Bean

A timeless classic. Delicate vanilla bean cake layered with a silky, rich vanilla buttercream. Simple, elegant, and profoundly delicious.

Midnight Chocolate

An intense and decadent experience. Layers of rich, dark chocolate cake are paired with a whipped chocolate ganache for the ultimate chocolate lover.

Salted Caramel Crème

A perfect balance of sweet and salty. Our signature vanilla cake filled with house-made salted caramel and frosted with a light caramel crème.

Red Velvet Royale

Our take on a Southern classic. A moist, vibrant red velvet cake with a hint of cocoa, paired with a luxurious cream cheese frosting.

Lemon & Elderflower

A bright and floral delight. Zesty lemon cake infused with delicate elderflower syrup, finished with a light lemon buttercream.

Almond Amaretto

A sophisticated and nutty flavor. Tender almond cake soaked in Amaretto syrup, layered with a delicate almond-infused buttercream.

September's Seasonal Selections

A taste of early autumn, crafted with the best ingredients the season has to offer.

Spiced Apple Crumble

Warm apple spice cake with a cinnamon-oat crumble filling, frosted with a smooth brown sugar buttercream. A cozy, comforting classic.

Pumpkin Chai Latte

The essence of fall in a cake. Aromatic chai-spiced pumpkin cake layered with a creamy, espresso-infused buttercream.

Brown Butter Pecan

A rich and nutty delight. Brown butter cake studded with toasted pecans, complemented by a maple-infused cream cheese frosting.
