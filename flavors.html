From the Atelier

Beyond the oven, there's a world of creativity and tradition that shapes every cake we make. Here's a glimpse into the artistry and thoughtful details that define The Gilded Spoon.

Perfect Pairings

Great cake is even better with the right companion. Here are a few of our favorite suggestions:

  • For Fruity & Floral Cakes: Try a crisp, dry Prosecco or a sparkling Rosé. A non-alcoholic elderflower pressé or iced hibiscus tea is also a wonderful match.
  • For Rich & Chocolatey Cakes: A bold coffee or a smooth cold brew cuts through the richness beautifully. For an evening treat, a Cabernet Sauvignon or a stout can be a decadent pairing.
  • For Spiced & Nutty Cakes: A warm cup of chai, a spiced apple cider, or a smooth bourbon complement these flavors perfectly.

Fun Facts & Ingredients

Every flavor tells a story. Discover some intriguing details about our ingredients and the history that inspires our creations:

  • Ancient Origins: The tradition of birthday candles dates back to Ancient Greece, where cakes were lit to glow like the moon in honor of the goddess Artemis.
  • The True Red Velvet: The iconic red hue of Red Velvet cake originally came from a natural chemical reaction between acidic cocoa powder and buttermilk, not food dye!
  • Beyond the Ordinary: We meticulously source unique spices like Mahleb (from cherry stones) and Grains of Paradise (a peppery, citrusy spice) to create layers of complexity.
