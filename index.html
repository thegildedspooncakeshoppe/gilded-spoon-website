The Spine of the Brand

Meet Dimple!

I am a Founder, Author, and Former Nurse—but most importantly, I am a sensory designer for the beautiful mess of the human story.

The Gilded Spoon was born as my own sanctuary to navigate a chaotic world. I realized that the rhythmic structure of cake design wasn't just about dessert; it was Emotional Architecture.

I’ve traded clinical rounds for creative regulation, using my medical background to build tools that help you ground your nervous system, protect your energy, and finally stop performing. Let's start feeling, together.
