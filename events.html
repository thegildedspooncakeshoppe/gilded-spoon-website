Two Paths to a Perfect Event

Choose the creative journey that best fits your occasion.

The Paint by Cake Workshop

Rediscover the joy of Paint by Number with a delicious twist! In this unique workshop, your canvas is a cake and our signature buttercream is your paint.

Each guest receives a personal cake with a pre-printed edible design divided into numbered sections. Using a corresponding palette of colorful buttercreams and royal icing, you'll "paint" in the design to create a beautiful masterpiece. Choose from our seasonal/signature designs or upgrade to a custom image for a truly personal touch.

Perfect for: Joyful team-building, birthday parties, bridal showers, and creative celebrations.

The Flavor Personality Atelier

This unique cake decorating workshop is an immersive sensory journey to the heart of your own palate. The experience begins with a curated tasting and guided journaling to help each guest uncover their unique 'Flavor Personality.'

You will then use your newfound profile to design and decorate a stunning cake that is a true expression of your personal story. For an even more personalized touch, this experience can be enhanced with a custom edible image for an additional fee.

Perfect for: Unique date nights, milestone celebrations, client appreciation, and sophisticated foodie experiences.
