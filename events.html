Two Paths to a Perfect Event

Choose the creative journey that best fits your occasion.

The Paint by Cake Workshop

Rediscover the joy of Paint by Number with a delicious twist! In this unique workshop, your canvas is a personal cake and our signature buttercream is your paint.

Each guest receives a personal cake with a pre-printed design. Using your palette, you'll "paint" in the sections to create a masterpiece.

Perfect for: Team building, birthday parties, and creative showers.

The Gilded Palate Atelier

An immersive sensory journey. We begin with a curated tasting board to help each guest uncover their unique "Flavor Personality."

You will then use your profile to design a cake that is a tangible expression of your story. This is "Emotional Architecture" in its most delicious form.

Perfect for: Date nights, client appreciation, and VIP milestone celebrations.

The Paint by Cake Process

1. You arrive at a pre-styled tablescape.
2. You receive your personal cake 'canvas' with a numbered edible design.
3. Using professional buttercreams and palettes, you follow the guide to 'paint' your design.
4. We provide boxes for everyone to take their masterpieces home.

×

The Atelier Journey

1. Curated Tasting: We begin with a sensory flavor exploration.
2. Guided Journaling: You identify your 'Flavor Personality' using the 5-Layer Framework.
3. Architecture: You design a bespoke cake based on your internal story.
4. Execution: You decorate a high-end centerpiece using luxury techniques.
