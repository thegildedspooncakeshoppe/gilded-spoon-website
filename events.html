Two Paths to a Perfect Event

Choose the creative journey that best fits your occasion.

The Paint by Cake Workshop

Unleash your inner artist in a workshop where the canvas is cake and the paint is our signature buttercream! This experience is all about fun, creativity, and the joy of making something beautiful that you can also eat.

Guided by our expert instructors, you'll learn essential decorating techniques before letting your imagination run wild. It's the perfect activity for team-building, client entertainment, or celebrating a special milestone with pure, hands-on fun.

Perfect for: Joyful team-building, birthday parties, bridal showers, and creative celebrations.
