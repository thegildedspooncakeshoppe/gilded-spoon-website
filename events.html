Two Paths to a Perfect Event

Choose the creative journey that best fits your occasion.

The Paint by Cake Workshop

Rediscover the joy of Paint by Number with a delicious twist! In this unique workshop, your canvas is a cake and our signature buttercream is your paint.

Each guest receives a personal cake with a pre-printed edible design divided into numbered sections. Using a corresponding palette of colorful buttercreams and royal icing, you'll "paint" in the design to create a beautiful masterpiece.

Perfect for: Joyful team-building, birthday parties, bridal showers, and creative celebrations.

The Gilded Palate: A Cake Design Atelier

This unique cake decorating workshop is an immersive sensory journey. The experience begins with a curated tasting and guided journaling to help each guest uncover their unique 'Flavor Personality.'

You will then use your newfound profile to design and decorate a stunning cake that is a true expression of your personal story.

Perfect for: Unique date nights, milestone celebrations, client appreciation, and sophisticated foodie experiences.
