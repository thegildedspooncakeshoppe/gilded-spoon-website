The Joyful Journey

The Paint by Cake Workshop

In this unique workshop, your canvas is a personal cake and our signature buttercream is your paint. We've removed the pressure of "perfection" and replaced it with low-friction creativity.

Each guest receives a pre-printed edible design divided into numbered sections. Using your corresponding color palette, you'll "paint" your masterpiece while grounding your nervous system in the sensory rhythm of the frosting.

Perfect for: Team regulation, bridal showers, and creative birthday celebrations.

The Immersive Journey

The Gilded Palate:
A Cake Design Atelier

This experience is an immersive sensory journey through my Proprietary 5-Layer Framework. We begin with a curated tasting board to help each guest uncover their unique "Flavor Personality."

You will then use your profile to design and decorate a stunning cake that is a tangible expression of your story. This is "Emotional Architecture" in its most delicious form.

Perfect for: Sophisticated date nights, client appreciation, and VIP milestone celebrations.
